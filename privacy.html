This Privacy Statement governs our data collection, processing and usage practices. It also describes your choices regarding the use, access and correction of your personal information.

We also archive all historical versions of this Privacy Statement.


Data protection principles


Under the GDPR, there are six data protection principles that Epic Trade must comply with. These provide that the personal information we hold about you must be:


  • processed lawfully, fairly and in a transparent manner;
  • collected only for legitimate purposes that have been clearly explained to you and not further processed in a way that is incompatible with those purposes;
  • adequate, relevant and limited to what is necessary in relation to those purposes;
  • accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date;
  • kept in a form which permits your identification for no longer than is necessary for those purposes;
  • processed in a way that ensures appropriate security of the data.
  • Epic Trade is responsible for, and must be able to demonstrate compliance with, these principles. This is called accountability.
